<p><b>Beladevipur</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Village_development_committee" title="Village development committee">village development committee</a> in <a href="/wiki/Kailali_District" title="Kailali District">Kailali District</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Seti_Zone" title="Seti Zone">Seti Zone</a> of western <a href="/wiki/Nepal" title="Nepal">Nepal</a>. At the time of the <a href="/wiki/1991_Nepal_census" title="1991 Nepal census">1991 Nepal census</a> it had a population of 5006 living in 621 individual households.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
